Scrolling text video maker

Usage example: /runtext/?text=desired_text

You can also use these parameters:

Font size. Default: font_size=55

Image size in pixels. Default: image_size=100,100

Background color and text color in rgb. Default: bg_color=0,0,0 text_color=255,255,255

Duration in seconds. Default: d=3

Direction of a text, either 'left', or 'right'. Default: dir=left
